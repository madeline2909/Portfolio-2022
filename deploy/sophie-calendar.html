About
Sophie

Sophie is a duck who loves sunshine and exploring her world of bees and flowers. She may seem like a shy and quiet duck, but she is really good at making friends and has a very big heart.

The
calendar

This Sophie calendar project consists of 12 illustrations of Sophie in each month along with a set of stickers. The calendar comes in two sizes: a full-sized spiral-bound desk calendar and a convenient set of small 6x9cm calendar cards that can be kept behind your clear phone case.
