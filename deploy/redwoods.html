The
concept

Today’s younger generation experiences firsthand pandemics, climate change, global warming, and too many more environmental issues that have been threatening their future. There has been a lot of stress and attention to the importance of young people saving this world, to the point that is too much. With the hope of lessening the burden on the younger generation while still raising awareness about the environment, this magazine was created.

The
publication

Redwoods is a magazine for young adults that focuses on the environment with a touch of lifestyle. Environmental issues can be heavy and hard to grasp at first, which is why this publication was designed to be a more positive and approachable way of learning. In Redwoods, you can get information about environmental campaigns, projects, as well as the simplest ways to live a greener lifestyle. The layouts are experimental with bright colorful images to make the magazine look more like a lifestyle journal while still encouraging learning.
