My Childhood Story

animation
\ illustration

Adobe Photoshop
\ Adobe After Effects

The
song

“My Childhood Story” by Sandeul is a bittersweet ballad reminiscing about one’s innocent and pure childhood days. The lyrics talk about how time moves on and everything fades away, even your memories of precious moments in the past.

The
animation

While creating this animation, I tried to capture the song’s bittersweet message through the clear and bright color palette with cinematographic lighting. I first came up with the script, compositions, and keyframes. I then created frame-by-frame illustrations in Photoshop and assembled the scenes together in After Effect.
