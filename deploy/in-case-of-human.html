The
concept

In this world, extroverts are the “norm”. People are expected to communicate and make conversation everywhere they go, but for introverts, it’s a stressful and tiring process that they all wish to escape from. Based on that concept, this fun package was created with the hope to be a whimsical, light-hearted, and stress-free experience for any introverted person struggling with making conversation.

The
kit

This is a survival kit for introverts who are tired of unwanted small talks and meaningless conversations.
This kit includes one hoodie, one pair of sunglasses, one booklet, and two packs of chewing gums, all specifically designed for introverts.
